## What we teach

Not "prompt engineering tips." The mental model: how agents actually work, what they're good at, what to watch for, and how to measure whether they're pulling their weight. Engineers learn the SDK patterns; ops leads learn the systems view; execs learn the questions to ask.

## How we ship

Four weeks, three cohorts. Every workshop ends with something built — a prompt pattern, an eval set, a dashboard — so the team walks away with artifacts, not just notes.

## What you get

A team that owns the next six workflows without a call back to us.
