## The problem

You have three BI tools, four dashboards per department, and nobody trusts the numbers. Decisions get made on gut feel because the data path is too long.

## How we ship

We consolidate your event layer, normalize definitions with the team in the room, and land one dashboard per function that leadership actually opens. Then an agent watches the metrics and writes you a weekly note — what moved, what didn't, what to ask about in Monday's standup.

## What you get

A warehouse that's the one version of truth, tracking that's trustable, and a signal layer that surfaces what matters without anyone pulling a report.
