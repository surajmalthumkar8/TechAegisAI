## What "agentic" actually means here

An agent is a process that reads context, picks an action from a menu, takes it, and checks its work. Done right, it replaces the tedious parts of knowledge work — triage, summarization, follow-through — without needing a person to prompt it.

## How we ship

We start with the workflow closest to revenue: sales enablement, support triage, or invoice-to-cash. We build the agent behind a queue with a human checkpoint for the first two weeks. Once the eval harness shows 95%+ agreement with the human, we turn the checkpoint off for the easy cases.

## What you get

A system that handles the boring 80% so your team can focus on the 20% that compounds.
