## The challenge

Reps were spending two hours a day on pre-call research — LinkedIn, funding news, job postings, past tickets — and still showing up to calls without the context that actually matters. The SDR team had asked for three new hires; the CRO wanted leverage instead.

## What we shipped

An agent sitting on top of the CRM that pulls signal from ten sources, writes a two-paragraph briefing, and drafts the first outreach in the rep's voice. The rep reviews, edits, and sends. The whole pre-call loop went from two hours to fifteen minutes.

We also wired up a closed-loop eval: reps rate every briefing, and the agent retrains on the ratings weekly. Quality went up every sprint.

## The outcome

Pipeline up 30% without a new hire. New reps closed their first deal 40% faster. The SDR manager stopped asking about headcount and started asking about quota.

> Our reps now close deals with context they never had. Pipeline is up 30% without a single new hire.
> — VP Sales
